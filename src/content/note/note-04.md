## Using a notebook to dump my thoughts

I've been watching a lot of people on social media using notebooks as
journals, sketch books, and more. These are called traveler notebooks,
which compiles a lot of different things into one. I have decided to
give it a try since I always have a lot of thoughts on my mind and
might be worth dumping them to ease my mind.
